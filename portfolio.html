Chassis Sub-frame


View CAD here

Details:

  • Designed using Solid Edge 2023.
  • Sub-frame is a structural component of a vehicle that is bolted to main unibody to carry engine, drivetrain, suspension and steering rack.
  • Other function is to distribute high local loads over a wider area of unibody structure.
  • Sub-frame also isolate vibration and harshness from the rest of the vehicle body.
  • Typically developed using sheet metal stamping or hydroforming, extrusion or casting process.
  • For this design, I designed the parts with consideration for the sheet metal stamping process.

Centrifugal Pump


View CAD here

Details:

  • Designed using Solid Edge 2023.
  • I designed this centrifugal pump according to API 610 specification where applicable.
  • Type OH1 pumps are foot-mounted, single-stage overhung pumps.
  • As a note, while OH1 pumps are described in API610, it does not meet all the requirements of the standard.
  • For this design, I used a closed type impeller with wear rings, a single spring mechanical seal and API Plan 11 flush line.
  • The bearing housing is fitted with a constant level oiler,one radial bearing that support radial loads perpendicular to the pump shaft, and one angular thrust bearing that accommodate axial and radial loads.

Diesel Generator Skid


View CAD here

Details:

  • Designed using Solid Edge 2023.
  • A preliminary design of a 60kVA diesel generator set skid for use in ATEX Zone 2 area.
  • ATEX Zone 2 area = hazardous area classified as an atmosphere where a mixture of air and flammable substances in the form of gas, vapor or mist is not likely to occur, but if it does occur will persist for a short period.
  • I designed the skid frame according to DNVGL-ST-E271 specification.
  • Powertrain: Perkins 1104D-44T diesel engine.
  • Alternator: Stamford AVK.
  • Explosion proof air inlet and oil vent flame arrestor, spark arrestor, exhaust gas heat exchanger, engine starter are chosen for this design.

CDLR Conveyor Line


View CAD here

Details:

  • Designed using Solid Edge 2023.
  • A basic design of a Chain Driven Live Roller (CDLR) Conveyor Line system.
  • Chain driven live roller conveyor system is a type of mechanical conveying system where a series of rollers are connected together with a chain.
  • CDLR systems are ideal for transporting medium to heavy loads (e.g. loaded pallets, tires, drums and totes) at controlled speeds.
  • For this design, I combined a linear conveyor line and a turntable conveyor.
  • The turntable conveyor system consists of a round platform connected to an external gear slewing bearing, which is rotated using a gearmotor.

Foam Proportioning Piping Skid


View CAD here

Details:

  • Designed using Solid Edge 2022.
  • A basic design of a foam proportioning system piping skid based on NFPA 11 standard, and is used in industrial and petrochemical firefighting.
  • For this design, I used balanced pressure proportioning that utilize a foam concentrate pump and a diaphragm-operated pressure balancing valve to provide the correct foam concentrate injection percentage automatically.
  • The external gear pump is fitted with a TEFC induction motor that allows the pump to produce flow of up to 75 gpm.
  • The skid frame is designed to be easily transportable with 2 forklift holes and 4 lifting eye on each corner.
